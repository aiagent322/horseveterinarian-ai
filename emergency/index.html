🚨 When Every Minute Counts

Emergency Guide

Educational protocols for equine emergencies — colic, wounds, eye injuries, choke, and lacerations. Know what to do before your veterinarian arrives.

In any equine emergency, your first action should be to contact your licensed equine veterinarian. These guides provide educational context for what to do while awaiting professional care — they do not replace veterinary examination and treatment. Have your veterinarian's emergency contact number saved before you need it. Find an equine vet →
